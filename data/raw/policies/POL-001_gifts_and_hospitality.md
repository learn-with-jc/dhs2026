# POL-001: Gifts and Hospitality Policy
**Version:** 2.1
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
**Category:** gifts
**Owner:** Global Compliance Office

## 1. Purpose
This policy governs the giving and receiving of gifts and hospitality
by employees and contractors to ensure compliance with anti-bribery
laws and ethical business standards.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties acting
on behalf of the organisation.

## 3. Thresholds

### 3.1 Employee Recipients
- Single gift limit: **USD 150 per occasion**
- Annual cumulative limit per recipient: **USD 500 per calendar year**
- Gifts exceeding USD 150 require **manager approval and documentation**
- Annual cumulative tracking is mandatory

### 3.2 External Recipients (Private Sector)
- Single gift limit: **USD 150 per occasion**
- Annual cumulative limit per recipient: **USD 500 per calendar year**
- Gifts to customers require **manager approval at any value**

### 3.3 Public Sector Recipients
- Gifts to public sector employees or government officials
  are **subject to country-specific limits** (see POL-005)
- Default limit where no country-specific rule exists: **USD 25**
- Cash-equivalent gifts (gift cards, vouchers, prepaid cards)
  to public officials are **PROHIBITED** without exception

## 4. Prohibited Items
The following are prohibited regardless of value:
- Cash or cash-equivalent gifts to public officials
- Gifts to individuals involved in active procurement decisions
- Gifts that could be construed as inducements or bribes
- Alcohol to recipients in jurisdictions where prohibited

## 5. Approval Requirements
| Gift Value         | Approval Required         |
|--------------------|---------------------------|
| Under USD 50       | No approval required      |
| USD 50 – USD 150   | Manager approval          |
| Over USD 150       | Director + Compliance     |
| To public official | Legal + VP minimum        |

## 6. Documentation
All gifts over USD 50 must be logged in the Gift Register within
5 business days of giving.

## 7. Exceptions
- Branded promotional items under USD 25 (pens, notebooks, mugs)
  do not require approval
- Dowlis platform charitable donation vouchers are exempt
  (see Exception Catalog EXC-003)
- Mobility devices with documented medical need are exempt
  (see Exception Catalog EXC-007)
