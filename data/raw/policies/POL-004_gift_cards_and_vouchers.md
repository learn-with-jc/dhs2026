# POL-004: Gift Cards and Vouchers Policy
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
**Category:** gift_cards
**Owner:** Global Compliance Office

## 1. Purpose
Regulates the purchase and distribution of gift cards, prepaid
cards, and vouchers due to their cash-equivalent nature and
elevated compliance risk.

## 2. General Rules
- All gift card purchases require **manager pre-approval**
- A complete recipient list with names and affiliations
  must be submitted before purchase
- Gift cards may not be given anonymously

## 3. Per-Recipient Limits (Private Sector)
- Maximum per recipient per occasion: **USD 150**
- Annual maximum per recipient: **USD 500**
- Values matching these ceilings require documentation

## 4. Public Sector and Government Recipients

### 4.1 Prohibition
Gift cards, prepaid cards, and cash-equivalent vouchers are
**PROHIBITED** for:
- Government employees at any level
- Public sector officials
- Regulatory agency staff
- Any individual whose organisation receives government funding

### 4.2 No Exceptions for Cash-Equivalent to Public Officials
This prohibition has **no monetary threshold exception**.
Even a USD 5 gift card to a public official is prohibited.

## 5. Approved Exception: Dowlis Platform
Charitable donation vouchers issued through the **Dowlis
Corporate Platform** are exempt from gift card restrictions
provided:
- The voucher is redeemable only for approved charities
- Distribution is through the Dowlis platform exclusively
- The recipient selects their own charity
- Documentation is retained (Exception Catalog EXC-003)

## 6. Other Approved Platforms
Any new platform seeking exemption status must be approved
by Global Compliance before use.

## 7. Prohibited Gift Cards
- Prepaid Visa/Mastercard/Amex (cash-equivalent)
  to public sector recipients: **PROHIBITED without exception**
- Gift cards to individuals in active procurement roles
- Gift cards without documented recipients
