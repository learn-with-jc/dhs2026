# POL-003: Meals and Entertainment Policy
**Version:** 3.0
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
**Category:** meals
**Owner:** Global Compliance Office

## 1. Purpose
Sets limits on business meals and entertainment expenses
to maintain ethical standards while supporting legitimate
business relationship building.

## 2. Per-Head Spending Limits

### 2.1 Internal Only (Employees and Contractors)
- Standard limit: **USD 75 per person**
- Includes food, non-alcoholic beverages, and service charge
- Excludes venue hire (charged separately under facilities)

### 2.2 With External Guests (Private Sector)
- Standard limit: **USD 150 per person**
- Applies when at least one external private sector guest attends
- Prior approval required when total event cost exceeds **USD 500**

### 2.3 With Public Sector Guests
- Refer to **POL-005** for country-specific per-head limits
- Prior approval from Legal required for any meal with
  known public officials
- Default cap where no country rule exists: **USD 50 per person**

## 3. Total Event Thresholds
| Total Event Cost    | Prior Approval Required            |
|---------------------|-------------------------------------|
| Under USD 100       | None                                |
| USD 100 – USD 500   | Manager approval post-event         |
| Over USD 500        | **Prior written approval required** |
| Over USD 2,000      | Director approval + Compliance log  |
| Over USD 5,000      | VP approval required                |

## 4. Documentation Requirements
- Guest list with organisation affiliation required
  for all events over USD 200
- Business purpose must be documented
- Receipts required for all amounts

## 5. Prohibited Expenses
- Entertainment at adult venues or casinos
- Events with no documented business purpose
- Alcohol expenses billed separately from meal
  without specific approval

## 6. Frequency
Meals with the same external party more than **once per month**
require manager notification and documentation.
